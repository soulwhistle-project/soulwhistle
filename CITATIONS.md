## Research Quality Notes

### Strengths:
- Multiple peer-reviewed studies across major journals
- Neurophysiological measurements (EEG, MEG, fMRI)
- Controlled experimental designs
- Replication attempts across independent labs

### Limitations:
- Small sample sizes (often n=24 or less)
- Methodological inconsistencies between studies
- Contradictory findings (especially for memory and vigilance)
- Individual variation is significant
- Publication bias (negative results underreported)

### Current Scientific Consensus (2025):
1. Binaural beats CAN produce measurable EEG changes
2. Effects are HIGHLY INDIVIDUAL and often subtle
3. Anxiety reduction has most consistent evidence
4. Cognitive enhancement claims are overstated
5. More research needed with standardized protocols
